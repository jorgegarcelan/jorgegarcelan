## 👋 About me

I've been fascinated by **AI & tech** since I was little. My path took me from a **Data Science & Engineering** degree at UC3M, to an exchange year at **Georgia Tech**, to research labs and **AWS** — always chasing problems where AI can genuinely help.

Today I focus on **responsible, real-world AI**: models that ship, systems that scale, and a startup that helps students find their place in the world. Off the clock you'll find me go-karting 🏎️, gaming, travelling, or dreaming up the next thing to build.

> *I turn messy, real-world research into AI people trust — models that ship, systems that scale, and a startup that helps students find their place in the world.*
